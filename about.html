Meet Maria

Maria is a working mom of three. She is always on the go. Maria is constantly juggling many responsibilities including work, family, and self care. Sometimes it can lead a busy mom to burn herself out!

We created "Foodie" For a mother like Maria who can't find the time in her busy schedule. In the world of a mom there are many things you have to handle.

Why not not let "Foodie" plan your meals for you!

"Foodie" gives you menu options throughout the week so you don't have to come up with the ideas for dinner tonight. Plus, we'll create a grocery list that you can access right from your phone. All you have to to do is select, prepare, cook, and serve, giving you more time to do the things that matter most to you.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide thoughtfully curated menu options and easy-to-follow recipes. This allows our users to effortlessly prepare delicious meals throughout the week by taking the stress out of dinner planning.

"Foodie" empowers you to spend more quality time with your loved ones and focus on the things that matter most.

We strive to bring joy and simplicity to your kitchen, one meal at a time.
